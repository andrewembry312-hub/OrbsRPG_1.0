# Fighter Card Collection System - User Guide

## Overview
The Fighter Card Collection system allows you to collect, level up, and manage fighter cards that power your guard and ally slots.

## Accessing the Collection

1. **Open Character Panel** (Press `I` or click Inventory icon)
2. **Click the "ðŸŽ´ Collection" Tab**

## Collection Features

### Card Display
Each card shows:
- **Fighter Image** - Visual representation
- **Level Badge** (Top-left) - Current card level (1-50)
- **Name** - Fighter card name
- **Rarity** - Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, or Legendary
- **Role** - DPS, Tank, or Healer
- **Stats**:
  - Total Kills
  - Current XP / Max XP
- **XP Progress Bar** - Visual XP progress
- **Equipped Status** - Shows if card is currently equipped

**Filter by Role:**

### Card Actions

#### Equip Card
1. Click **"EQUIP"** button on a card
2. Choose which slot to equip it to (must match role)
3. Existing card in slot will be unequipped
4. Equipped cards show gold "EQUIPPED" badge

**Note:** Can only equip to unlocked slots that match the card's role (DPS/Tank/Healer)

#### Sell Card
1. Click **"SELL"** button
2. Confirm sale dialog
3. Receive gold based on rarity:
   - Common: 50g
   - Uncommon: 150g
   - Rare: 500g
   - Epic: 2,000g
   - Legendary: 10,000g

**Note:** Cannot sell equipped cards (unequip first)

## Obtaining Cards

### Starting Cards
- New games start with **3 random Common cards**

### Card Drops
- **5% chance** from any enemy kill
- **100% chance** from boss kills (guaranteed Rare or better)

### Buy Card Packs
Visit **Marketplace â†’ Fighter Cards Tab**:

1. **Common Pack** (100g)
   - 3 random cards
   - Mostly common, chance for uncommon/rare

2. **Rare Pack** (500g)
   - 3 cards
   - **Guaranteed** at least 1 Rare or better

3. **Epic Pack** (2,000g)
   - 3 cards
   - **Guaranteed** at least 1 Epic or better

## Card Progression

### Leveling System
- Cards gain **10 XP per kill** when equipped in a slot
- XP requirement increases exponentially
- **Max Level:** 50
- Higher level = stronger fighter when spawned

### Level Scaling
- Each level above 1 grants **+10% stats**
- Example: Level 10 card = +90% to all stats

### Rarity Multipliers
Applied to all equipment stats:
- Common: 1.0x (base)
- Uncommon: 1.2x
- Rare: 1.5x
- Epic: 2.0x
- Legendary: 3.0x

## Strategy Tips

1. **Equip Higher Level Cards** - Level matters more than rarity early on
2. **Match Roles** - Can only equip DPS cards to DPS slots, etc.
3. **Farm XP** - Keep cards equipped to level them up passively
4. **Save Legendaries** - Very rare, high sell value, powerful multipliers
5. **Sell Duplicate Commons** - Convert extras to gold for packs
6. **Balance Your Collection** - Have good cards for all 3 roles
